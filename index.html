Profile

About Sanjeev

Sanjeev Singhai is a seasoned business leader and communication specialist with more than three decades of experience in media, marketing, and technology-led ventures.

He is the Founder & CEO of Wellnessta, a wellness-tech platform that digitises local salons, spas, gyms, yoga, and wellness centres through cloud-based appointment, inventory, coupon and payroll solutions along with an integrated consumer marketplace.

Before Wellnessta, Sanjeev held leadership roles across Amar Ujala, DB Corp (Dainik Bhaskar Group), and The Brand Power Company, where he helped launch and scale HomeTesterClub.com across multiple markets.

He has also contributed to India’s MSME ecosystem as National Vice President – Organisational Development & Governance at MSME & Start-ups Forum Bharat, working closely with entrepreneurs and growth-stage businesses.
